# Site Architecture — GaryMariner.com

## Core Objective

Design a high-converting, premium digital experience that positions Gary Mariner as an AI Systems Architect for Business Operations.

The site must:
- establish authority quickly
- communicate value clearly
- demonstrate proof through real outcomes
- guide users toward booking a consultation

---

## Primary User Journey

1. User lands on homepage
2. Understands positioning within seconds
3. Gains trust through authority and proof
4. Understands services and value
5. Sees real-world results (case study)
6. Feels differentiated perspective
7. Converts (books a consultation or reaches out)

---

## Site Structure

### Pages

- Home
- About
- Services
- Case Studies
- Contact / Book

---

## Homepage Architecture

### Section 1 — Hero (Attention + Positioning)

**Goal:** Immediate clarity and authority

Content:
- Positioning statement
- Clear headline (short, strong)
- Supporting statement
- Primary CTA (Book)
- Secondary CTA (Work)

---

### Section 2 — Authority Strip (Trust Anchors)

**Goal:** Build immediate credibility

Content:
- 3–5 authority indicators:
  - Operations leadership
  - AI systems implementation
  - measurable business results
  - cross-functional experience

Must feel:
- specific
- grounded
- not generic

---

### Section 3 — Positioning Block (Problem → Solution)

**Goal:** Clarify what Gary actually does

Structure:
- Problem: operational friction, complexity
- Shift: systems thinking over tools
- Solution: AI-assisted systems + infrastructure

---

### Section 4 — Services Preview (Offer Clarity)

**Goal:** Make the offer tangible

Structure:
- 4 service cards:
  1. AI Systems Strategy
  2. Digital Infrastructure
  3. Operations Optimization
  4. Communication Systems

Each card:
- outcome-focused
- clearly targeted
- no vague language

---

### Section 5 — Proof Section (Credibility Unlock)

**Goal:** Prove capability with real results

Content:

#### Digital Harmony (Primary)
- 70% reduction in live agent dependency
- 90% increase in customer satisfaction
- revenue growth from $75K → $130K

#### Radco (Secondary)
- occupancy increase (~80% → 93%)
- work order reduction (360 → 23)

Important:
- must be visible
- must be scannable
- must feel real

---

### Section 6 — Case Study Preview (Narrative Proof)

**Goal:** Show transformation, not just metrics

Feature:
**Digital Harmony Case Study**

Structure:
- Problem
- Strategy
- Execution
- Result

CTA:
- View Full Case Study

---

### Section 7 — Operating Philosophy (Differentiation)

**Goal:** Separate Gary from generic consultants

Content:
- systems > tools
- clarity > complexity
- strategy before execution

Tone:
- thoughtful
- confident
- not salesy

---

### Section 8 — Final CTA (Conversion)

**Goal:** Convert qualified visitors

Content:
- problem-based framing
- direct invitation

CTAs:
- Book a Consultation
- Start a Conversation

---

- No filler content

- Fast readability
- Strong visual pacing

- Strategic

- Credible
- Intentional

## What This Site Must Avoid

- Over-designed UI
- Empty buzzwords
