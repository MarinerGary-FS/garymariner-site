# **HOMEPAGE SCENE ARCHITECTURE.md**

## **GARYMARINER.COM — HOMEPAGE SCENE ARCHITECTURE V1**

---

# **EXPERIENCE THESIS**

The homepage is not a landing page.

It is:

# **an immersive executive editorial environment.**

The experience should progressively reveal:

* systems intelligence  
* organizational translation  
* technological sophistication  
* human-centered transformation  
* ecosystem depth  
* executive presence

The homepage should feel:  
directed,  
curated,  
cinematic,  
intellectually engaging.

The user journey should create:

* intrigue  
* intellectual curiosity  
* strategic trust  
* ecosystem gravity

---

# **EXPERIENCE STRUCTURE**

The homepage operates as:

# **6 cinematic scenes.**

Each scene has:

* emotional purpose  
* narrative purpose  
* interaction philosophy  
* environmental behavior  
* visual pacing  
* conversion psychology

---

# **SCENE 1 — ENTRY INTO THE SYSTEM**

## **Narrative Purpose**

Disrupt expectations immediately.

The user must instantly understand:

“This is not a standard personal brand website.”

This scene establishes:

* atmosphere  
* intrigue  
* technological sophistication  
* executive presence  
* environmental intelligence

---

# **Emotional Target**

The visitor should feel:

* curious  
* visually engaged  
* intellectually alert  
* slightly disoriented in a good way

The emotional reaction should be:

“What exactly am I entering?”

---

# **Visual Behavior**

## **Environment**

The environment should feel:

* dimensional  
* alive  
* cinematic  
* infrastructural

Potential visual systems:

* ambient environmental lighting  
* layered depth  
* atmospheric gradients  
* subtle infrastructure motion  
* spatial typography  
* cinematic shadows  
* environmental particles/fogging  
* restrained motion layering

---

# **Motion Philosophy**

Motion should:

* awaken slowly  
* respond intelligently  
* feel environmental

Avoid:

* rapid movement  
* flashy transitions  
* aggressive animation

The environment should feel:  
aware,  
not performative.

---

# **Typography Strategy**

Large-scale editorial typography.

Minimal copy.

Typography should feel:

* confident  
* intentional  
* cinematic  
* intellectually sharp

Avoid:

* overexplaining  
* introducing Gary immediately  
* generic positioning statements

---

# **Information Density**

Extremely low.

The opening scene is:  
atmosphere first,  
interpretation second.

---

# **User Goal**

Keep the visitor scrolling.

Curiosity is the conversion mechanism.

---
